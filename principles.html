Principles behind the Fiilis Manifesto

We follow these principles:

Our top priority is to have good Fiilis with continuous delivery of valuable software.

We welcome changing requirements, even late in development, as they provide opportunities to infuse more Fiilis into our products.

We deliver working software frequently when it ready for the maximum Fiilis.

Developers must work freely with Fiilis not impeded by Business people.

We believe in empowering motivated individuals within an environment designed to boost Fiilis, providing them with the support they need to spread positivity and Fiilis.

The most efficient and effective method of conveying information to and within a development team is face-to-face conversation.

Fiilis is the primary measure of progress in software development.

Fiilis processes promote sustainable development. The sponsors, developers, and users should be able enjoy the process indefinitely.

Continuous attention to technical excellence and good design is essential for maintaining and enhancing the Fiilis experienced by all stakeholders.

Simplicity is crucial for maximizing Fiilis in our work, as it allows us to focus on delivering what truly brings joy and satisfaction.

The best architectures, requirements, and designs emerge from Fiilis.

At regular intervals, the team reflects on how to enhance the Fiilis by tuning and adjusting behavior to amplify positivity and satisfaction.
