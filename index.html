Manifesto for Fiilis Software Development

We are uncovering better fiilis of developing software by doing it and helping others do it. Through this work we have come to value:

Good Fiilis over Individuals and interactions

Good Fiilis over Working Product

Good Fiilis over Customer Collaboration

Good Fiilis over Responding to change

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the Fiilis on the left more.
